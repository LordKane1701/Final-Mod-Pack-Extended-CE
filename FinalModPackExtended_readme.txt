1.0: Soldier nationality flags. All enemies always spawn regardless of nodes. Extra information in the Ufopaedia. Alien bases are created in or near the infiltrated country. New armor properties (dodge, unarmed attacks, energy regeneration, etc.) and item properties (new damage types, CQC rules, etc.). Dynamic psi formulas. Added Quick Draw inventory slot. Increased max view distance to 40. AI can use explosives from turn 1. Hovertanks have better nightvision. Custom base names. Snakemen slowly regenerate. Visual damage indicator (by Mistar Red). Unit status indicators (graphics by Captain Corkscrew and Ivan Dogovich). Melee reactions. Other features. More complex TU costs for moving and reloading items.
